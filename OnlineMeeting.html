About SRIIT

Introduction and History

ShriRam Institute of information Technology(SRIIT) was established by Dayal Seva Evam Shiksha Samiti,Morena in the year 2007. The Samiti is  running two other colleges viz., ShriRam College Of Engineering & Management (SRCEM), established in the  1998 and ShriRam College Of  Pharmacy(SRCP) established in the year 2003. SRIIT become the third venture of the Society. Being a part of the ShriRam Group of Colleges, SRIIT is also nestled in  green and verdant environment. It owns an attractive and splendid building constructed on land spread over an area of 10 acres. Surrounded by lush lawns and trees, the institute provides a perfectly conducive and healthy environment for the teaching learning process. The institute building is an example of super architecture with vide corridors, spacious and well ventilated classrooms, a rich computerized library with all modern facilities.

Objectives

  • To provide high quality engineering education in professional manner making them most suitable for industries and business houses.

  • To meet the growing demand from the industry and commerce, research and development establishment, educational institutions by producing technocrats of high professional knowledge and skills.

  • To enrich knowledge and skill of students ensuring that the same match with global demand of technical hands.

  • To ensure overall personality development of the students.

  • To expand horizons of knowledge through academic contribution with the help of highly qualified renowned core associates and visiting faculty.
